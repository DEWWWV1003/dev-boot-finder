Stud Type Guide

Different pitch surfaces demand different stud geometries. Know your surface — dominate the game.

7 Surface Types · 40+ Boot Models · Find Your Match
FG

Firm Ground

Natural grass — dry or slightly damp. The most commonly used stud type worldwide.

  • Conical + bladed mixed studs
  • TPU or carbon fibre plate
  • Premier League, La Liga, Champions League
Used by: Mbappé Haaland De Bruyne
SG

Soft Ground

Wet, muddy, waterlogged natural pitches. Long removable metal studs bite deep for grip.

  • Removable metal / hybrid studs
  • Anti-clog traction design
  • Winter UK/Northern Europe fixtures
Used by: Van Dijk Rashford Neuer
AG

Artificial Ground

3G / 4G rubber-crumb synthetic turf. AG-certified short studs spread load evenly to protect joints.

  • Short rubber or TPU studs (50–70 pegs)
  • AG-certified soleplate
  • Academy training, stadium 3G pitches
Used by: Mané Saka Ederson
TF

Turf

Hard AstroTurf, rubber granule surfaces, and tarmac cage pitches. Dense rubber nub outsole for grip without damage.

  • Dozens of tiny rubber nubs across full sole
  • No individual studs — flat rubber pattern
  • Cage football, Powerleague, 5-a-side
Used by: Thierry Henry Tobin Heath
IC

Indoor Court

Sports halls, futsal courts, wooden gym floors. Flat non-marking rubber sole for quick pivots without scuffing the surface.

  • Completely flat rubber sole (non-marking)
  • Low-profile silhouette
  • Futsal, indoor arenas, sports halls
Used by: Falcão (Futsal) Ferrão
HG

Hard Ground

Compact, sun-baked natural earth pitches common in South Asia, North Africa, and Middle East. Short firm rubber studs prevent jarring on impact.

  • Short compact rubber or TPU studs
  • Extra cushioning midsole
  • Local leagues, desert pitches, dry climates
Used by: Hakimi En-Nesyri
MG

Multi Ground

Hybrid stud layout designed to perform across FG, AG, and HG surfaces — ideal for players who train on different pitches all week.

  • Mixed conical + rubber hybrid studs
  • Versatile across 3+ surface types
  • Training, recreational, weekend players
Used by: Sadio Mané Gareth Bale
